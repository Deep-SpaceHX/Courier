Who We Are

Deep Space Hauling Express (DSHX) is a premier hauling and logistics corporation operating in the vast universe of New Eden. Founded by seasoned pilots with decades of combined experience, we strive to provide top-notch hauling services.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to redefine hauling and logistics in New Eden by providing fast, secure, and affordable services. We strive to empower pilots and corporations to focus on their goals while we handle their logistical needs.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises, ensuring your cargo arrives safely and on time.
  • Security: Trust is earned. We prioritize the safety and confidentiality of all assets.
  • Professionalism: Our team of experienced pilots operates with the highest level of dedication and expertise.

Our History

Since our inception, DSHX has grown to become a trusted name in the EVE Online community. With thousands of successful deliveries and a commitment to excellence, we continue to expand our services and improve our operations.
