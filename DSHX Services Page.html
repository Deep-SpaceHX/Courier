What We Offer

At Deep Space Hauling Express (DSHX), we specialize in reliable and secure hauling services across New Eden. Whether you’re an independent pilot or a large corporation, we have the expertise and resources to meet your logistical needs.

  • Fleet Support: Dedicated services for mining operations and other fleet activities.
